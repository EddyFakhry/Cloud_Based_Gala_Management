How we can help you

GoSwimIreland is an interactive cloud based application that automates host club event organization and minimizes paper based work.

  • Mission - To make Gala entries paperless
  • Functionality -Upload club files and download entry file
  • Audience - Swimming clubs

Take a look at what we offer

Organizing entry to a swimming GALA can be quite time consuming: Producing text forms, Analyzing the eligibility of swimmers for every event, Informing swimmers, Finalizing registration forms, etc...

GoSwimIreland can automate all these tasks down to a click of a button
